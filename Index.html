coffee recipe

items needed

  • 15 grams of coffeebean
  • coffee bean grinder
  • hario v60
  • v60 filter
  • gooseneck kettle
  • filter water
how to make
  1. 0.00 Add 2x coffee weight = 60 g of bloom water
  2. Swirl the coffee slurry until evenly mixed
  3. 0.45 Bloom for up to 45 s
  4. 1.15 Add water aiming for 60% of total brew weight = 300 g in the next 30 s (flow rate = 8 g/s)
  5. 1.45 Add water aiming for 100% of the total brew weight = 500 g in the next 30 s (flow rate = 6.66 g/s)
  6. Stir 1x clockwise and 1x anticlockwise with spoon
  7. Allow V60 to drain a little
  8. total draw time 3.30
